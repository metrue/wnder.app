Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 29, 2025

Wnder ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Wnder mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

Wnder requires access to your device's location to provide personalized audio stories about nearby places. We collect:

  • Precise location: GPS coordinates to identify nearby attractions and landmarks
  • Location history: Temporarily cached to improve story recommendations during your session

Location data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers.

1.2 Usage Data

We collect anonymized usage statistics to improve the App:

  • Features used (e.g., story playback, language selection)
  • App performance metrics
  • Error and crash reports

1.3 Subscription Information

If you subscribe to Wnder Pro, Apple handles all payment processing. We receive:

  • Subscription status (active, expired, trial)
  • Subscription tier (Basic, Pro)

We do not have access to your payment details, credit card information, or Apple ID.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized audio stories about nearby locations
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Manage your subscription status
  • Provide customer support
  • Fix bugs and improve stability

3. Third-Party Services

Wnder uses the following third-party services to deliver our features:

  • Content Services: For creating story content (text only, no personal data shared)
  • Voice Services: For high-quality audio narration
  • Apple MapKit: For displaying maps and nearby places
  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management

These services have their own privacy policies. We only share the minimum data necessary for functionality.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Location data is processed in real-time and not stored permanently
  • User preferences are stored locally on your device
  • We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission
  • No personal information is sold to third parties

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable location services in your device settings
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

6. Children's Privacy

Wnder is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The "Kid" expertise level provides age-appropriate content without collecting additional data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@wnder.app
  • Website: https://wnder.app

9. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union:

  • Legal basis for processing: Legitimate interest and consent
  • Data controller: Wnder
  • You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • Data is processed in the United States with appropriate safeguards

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
